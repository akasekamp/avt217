Draw With Me! ☺

First, open up Adobe XD.

The canvas size will be 1000 by 1000px.

We are going to make a triforce emblem from The Legend of Zelda (if you know what that is).

Make a 700 by 700px triangle, no fill, you can leave the outline.

Just go ahead and center that on the canvas.

This will be our outline to understand the layout.

Then make three 352 by 250 pixel triangles.

Make each smaller triangle a bright yellow color, you can leave the grey outline.

Then at the base of our big triangle, inside put two of the smaller triangle directly next to each other.

Then take your third smaller triangle and essentially stack it on top your two other small ones.

It should look like a big triangle, two on the bottom, one on top, and one upside white triangle in the middle.

You are done with the drawing so export it now as a PNG. Name it drawing.png so I can find it easily!

After you have exported the file into a PNG go ahead and email it to akasekam@masonlive.gmu.edu or you can find me on slack and DM me @ Allison Kasekamp and send it on there.

Good Luck!
